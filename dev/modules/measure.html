mad(referenceVideoData, distortedVideoData) Computes mean absolute deviation (MAD).
psnr(referenceVideoData, distortedVideoData) Computes Peak Signal to Noise Ratio (PSNR).

videobliinds_features(videoData) Computes Video Bliinds features.
